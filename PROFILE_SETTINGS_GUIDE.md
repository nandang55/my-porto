## üí° **Best Practices**

### **Slug vs Username:**
```
Slug: john-doe (lowercase, URL-friendly)
Username: JohnDoe (display, can have uppercase)

Why both?
- Slug: SEO-friendly URL
- Username: Better display (@JohnDoe)
```

### **Profile Completeness:**
```
Minimum (Basic):
‚úÖ Slug, Username, Name ‚Üí 30%

Good (Contactable):
‚úÖ + Email, Location, Bio ‚Üí 60%

Great (Professional):
‚úÖ + Skills, Experience, Social ‚Üí 85%

Perfect (SEO Optimized):
‚úÖ + Meta tags, Resume ‚Üí 100%
```

### **Writing Tips:**

**Bio:**
```
‚úÖ DO: "Passionate developer with 5 years experience..."
‚ùå DON'T: "I am a developer"

‚úÖ DO: Tell your story, achievements
‚ùå DON'T: Generic descriptions
```

**Tagline:**
